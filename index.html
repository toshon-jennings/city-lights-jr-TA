City Lights Jr. Tech Academy

Empowering Young Digital Citizens

City Lights Junior Tech Academy empowers grades 3–5 with real-world digital skills, a security-first mindset, and a Biblical worldview.

Students grow from digital novices into tech creators and cyber-citizens, through hands-on coding, website building, and cybersecurity projects—all on Chromebook-friendly platforms.

At every step, we're integrating our "5 Smooth Stones", helping every student develop habits of Consistency, Order, Vision, Work, and Excellence as they learn to shine online.

Then David took his staff in his hand; and he chose for himself five smooth stones from the brook, and put them in a shepherd's bag, in a pouch which he had, and his sling was in his hand. And he drew near to Goliath. -1 Samuel 17:40

  • Consistency: Safe and wise habits—practiced daily, in tech and life.
  • Order: Working with clarity, organization, and care.
  • Vision: Dreaming big, planning ahead, and seeing possibilities.
  • Work: Diligence, persistence, and teamwork in every project.
  • Excellence: Always offering our best, to God and others.
